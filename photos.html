Minato Mirai - みなとみらい

Located just 30 minutes from Tokyo, the Minato Mirai area is one of my favorite day trip locations.

Enoshima - 江ノ島

One of the closest beach areas to Tokyo, Enoshima provides scenic views and a tropical feel.

Showa Kinen Park - 昭和記念公園

Located in Tachikawa, Tokyo, Showa Kinen Park offers beautiful displays of foliage in the fall.

Illumination - イルミネーション

Despite the cold, dry weather, Tokyo really comes to life in the winter with lights scattered throughout the city, illuminating the night and attracting swarms of people to take pictures.
