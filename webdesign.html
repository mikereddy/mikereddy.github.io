DMA Power Review

View Site

Dean Morgan Academy (DMA) is a man-to-man English & French conversation school. With their own learning curriculum and use of technology in the lessons, the goal for this project was to provide an interface for students to easily find their current lesson's vocabulary and allow them to continue reviewing at home using Memrise. Through this website, over 400 users have registered and provided DMA with another USP to set themselves apart from the competitors.

Core Technology: Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, jQuery

Kics Document

View Site

Kics Document is a clothing company which was founded in Japan. The client's current web solution for their e-commerce site was not attracting many customers and overall was difficult to use. Using WordPress and WooCommerce, I created a website which would allow her to easily maintain her new products and provide an efficient, easy to use method for her customers to purchase her products and increase her revenue. The theme was built with Foundation, which was then converted into a WordPress theme.

Core Technology: Foundation, WordPress, PHP, WooCommerce, SEO
